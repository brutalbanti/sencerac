01
Initial Consult
$160
An initial naturopathy consultation takes around 90 minutes. During this session, you'll meet with a degree-qualified holistic health practitioner who will listen to your health concerns in detail. We take a comprehensive case history, exploring your diet, lifestyle, mental and emotional well-being, environment, and personal history. This thorough understanding helps us develop a personalized plan to address the root causes of your health issues and set you on the path to wellness.
02
Return Consult
$100
A follow-up consultation is a 45-minute session with your practitioner. We’ll check in on your progress and discuss how the treatment has been going. This is an opportunity to fine-tune your treatment plan, address any new or ongoing concerns, and ensure that your health is steadily improving. Regular follow-ups are essential to monitor your progress and make necessary adjustments to your plan.
03
Extended Return Consult
$120
This 60-minute session is for those who need more time with their practitioner. It’s ideal if you have a complex case, need to provide more information, or have experienced significant changes since your initial consultation. This extended time allows for a deeper dive into your health status and ensures that we can thoroughly address all aspects of your well-being.
04
Pathology Analysis and Report
$80-$110
This service is a valuable addition to your consultation. We’ll comprehensively review your pathology results to gain a better understanding of your health. You will receive a brief, easy-to-understand report explaining the findings, helping you to see a clearer picture of your health status. The price depends on the quantity of pathology to be analyzed. This analysis helps us tailor your treatment plan more precisely and effectively.
05
Acute Consult
$50
A quick consult designed for minor ailments such as a cough, cold, or a stressful week ahead. It can also provide extra support between your regular consultations. This 20-minute session can be done over the phone, via telehealth, or in person, perfect for addressing immediate health concerns that need timely attention without the need for a full consultation.
06
Free Discovery Call
Free
If you’re considering seeing a naturopath but are unsure if we are the right fit or how we can support you, book a free 5-10 minute discovery call to learn more. This call gives you the opportunity to ask questions, understand our approach, and determine how we can help you achieve your health goals.
